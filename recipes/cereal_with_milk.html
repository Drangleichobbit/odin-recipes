Cereal with milk

Description

Common breakfast all around the world. It was created and popularized by Kellogs with their Corn Flakes, but multiple brands and varieties exists nowadays.

Ingredients

  • A bowl or similar container
  • Cereal, of any brand and flavour
  • Milk

Preparation

  1. Open the box or bag of cereal.
  2. Fill the bowl with as much cereal as you want.
  3. Add any other ingredients you desire (fruit, nuts, honey).
  4. Add milk.
  5. Enjoy! :)
